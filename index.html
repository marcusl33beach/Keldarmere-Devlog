The Alliance of Six

At the center of the known world lies a wound in reality a Great Pit. To contain the nightmare within, six disparate races have forged an eternal pact. Surrounding the abyss stands the Hexagonal Citadel, where six mighty towers guard the perimeter of the world's most dangerous dungeon.

Dwarven Foundation

Architects of the deep bedrock. Their tower anchors the heavy iron defenses of the southern perimeter.

Elven Spires

Keepers of the ancient mana-glow. Their tower serves as the primary conduit for the city's magical shields.

Human Markets

The masters of logistics and diplomatic trade. Their sector ensures the alliance remains supplied for the long siege.

Gnomish Technocracy

Engineers of steam and gear. Their tower houses the clockwork mechanisms that operate the Great Gates.

Goblin Scrappers

Salvage experts who turn dungeon artifacts into volatile weaponry. Their sector is a forge of constant invention.

Orcish Wardens

Frontline veterans of the Abyss. Their tower acts as the central barracks for the warriors who walk the Pit's edge.
