The Great Containment

The Pit at the heart of Keldarmere was not born of nature, but of Ancient War. Decades of devastating magical conflict between the six races scarred the earth, opening a rift fueled by the very spells used to destroy one another. Now, that lingering evil magic has manifest into a living, shifting dungeon.

The Center Keep and its six mighty towers stand as the only barrier keeping that darkness from swallowing the world. While the fires of war still burn across the outer realms, the races maintain a cold, forced truce here—because if the Keep falls, everything falls.

Dwarven Foundation

Architects of the bedrock. Their tower anchors the heavy iron defenses of the southern perimeter.

Elven Spires

Keepers of the mana-glow. Their tower serves as the primary conduit for the city's magical shields.

Human Markets

The masters of logistics. Their sector ensures the alliance remains supplied during the eternal siege.

Gnomish Technocracy

Engineers of gear and steam. Their tower houses the mechanisms that operate the Great Gates.

Goblin Scrappers

Salvage experts who turn dungeon artifacts into volatile weaponry and rapid inventions.

Orcish Wardens

Frontline veterans of the Abyss. Their tower acts as the primary barracks for the warriors of the edge.

Beyond the Walls

Surrounding the fortress walls lies the Perimeter Town, a bustling hub for those who profit from the dungeon's riches. Beyond the town's safety lies the true world of Keldarmere—a landscape of "untouchable" wilderness and warring territories waiting to be explored.

Multi-Campaign Integration

Keldarmere features a persistent world with multiple active warfronts. Log into specific Campaigns with your characters to join localized battles, explore unique ruins, and change the tide of the ongoing war between the races.
